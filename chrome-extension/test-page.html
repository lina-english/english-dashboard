Day 12 · Meeting & Scheduling(模拟繁星课页)

今日学习要点:商务会议常用表达与排期沟通

1. 核心句子

我需要跟客户确认下周交付时间
I need to confirm the delivery schedule with the client next week.
这个方案需要得到 CEO 批准
This proposal requires approval from the CEO.
会议推迟到下周二
The meeting has been postponed to next Tuesday.

2. 字幕格式(兄弟元素)

请把议程发我一份Could you send me a copy of the agenda?
我们得赶快推进We need to move fast on this.

3. 词汇

  • 确认 | confirm
  • 批准 | approval
  • 议程 | agenda
  • 推进 | push forward
  • 延期 | postpone

4. 短语

  • 跟进进度 - follow up on the progress
  • 请求延期 - request an extension
  • 达成共识 - reach a consensus
