## 句子 Sentences

- 这个方案需要得到 CEO 批准 | This proposal requires approval from the CEO.
- 我们正在跟进仓库的库存问题 | We are following up on the inventory issue at the warehouse.

- 交付 | delivery
- 仓库 | warehouse
